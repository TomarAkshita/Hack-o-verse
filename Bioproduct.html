Bio-Produts or bio-based products are materials, chemicals and energy derived from renewable biological material. Biological resources include agriculture, forestry, and biologically derived waste, and there are many other renewable bioresource examples. Conventional bioproducts and emerging bioproducts are two broad categories used to categorize bioproducts. Bioproducts are products that are made from biomass. The meaning of biomass is any type of organic material that is available on a renewable basis.
